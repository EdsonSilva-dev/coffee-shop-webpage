Our Roots

Born from the mist of St. Stephen's Green.

The Dublin Editorial began with a simple belief: that a great cup of coffee should be accompanied by the right light, a comfortable chair, and the quiet rhythm of the city.

We source our beans from small-batch roasters who share our commitment to sustainability and fair practice. Every shot of espresso and every croissant is a tribute to the craftsmanship we found in the hidden lanes of Dublin 2.
